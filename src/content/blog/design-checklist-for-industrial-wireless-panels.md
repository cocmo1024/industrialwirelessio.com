Industrial wireless projects fail surprisingly often at the panel and enclosure layer, not just at the network layer.

That makes panel design checklists a strong evergreen content format. They are practical, link well to product categories, and align with commercial offers such as enclosure vendors, antenna suppliers, and remote I/O hardware sponsors.

A useful checklist should cover:

- enclosure environment and ingress protection
- antenna placement and cable routing
- power conditioning and backup
- grounding, shielding, and isolation
- field service access and labeling
