The LoRaWAN versus cellular question is rarely about one technology being universally better. It is about fit.

Range expectations, site density, maintenance access, data volume, latency tolerance, and service model all change the answer.

That is exactly why comparison content works so well on a site like `industrialwirelessio.com`: it attracts technically serious readers who are close to making infrastructure decisions.

The strongest long-term articles in this cluster should compare:

- topology and site density assumptions
- data volume and duty cycle
- power budget
- lifecycle maintenance
- service coverage and failure modes
