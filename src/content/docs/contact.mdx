Industrial Wireless I/O can be contacted for editorial communication, corrections, commercial inquiries, and policy-related questions.

## Primary contact

- Email: `editor@industrialwirelessio.com`

## Appropriate reasons to reach out

- report a correction or outdated technical statement;
- suggest an application, vendor, or hardware family that deserves coverage;
- ask about sponsorship, advertising, or partner inventory;
- raise a disclosure, transparency, or policy question.

## Response expectations

Messages are reviewed as capacity allows. Commercial contact does not purchase favorable coverage, preferred comparisons, or exclusion of competitors.
