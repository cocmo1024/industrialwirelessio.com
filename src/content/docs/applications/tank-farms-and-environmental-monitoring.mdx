# Tank Farms and Environmental Monitoring

These deployments usually depend on wide physical distribution, environmental exposure, and monitoring-first system goals. The architecture has to prioritize reliability, field serviceability, and data confidence over feature density.

## Typical pressures

- Long equipment spacing or difficult access
- Alarm visibility requirements with limited on-site staffing
- Power, enclosure, and serviceability constraints that dominate device choice
