Applications are the most useful way to organize industrial wireless content. Real deployments are shaped by field conditions, asset criticality, and maintenance realities.

## Focus

- Pump stations, utilities, environmental sites, and distributed assets
- Brownfield telemetry and remote monitoring scenarios
- Application pages that connect network paths, protocols, and hardware choices

This section is intended to become the core entry point for long-term site growth.
