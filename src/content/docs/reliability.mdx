Reliability is often the deciding factor in field-connected systems. This section is reserved for the practical issues that keep remote telemetry online over time.

## Focus

- Power resilience, antenna placement, enclosure planning, and maintenance
- Environmental conditions and failure points in distributed systems
- Design choices that improve uptime and reduce truck rolls

These pages are intended to become some of the highest-value operational reference material on the site.
