# Cellular vs LoRaWAN vs Satellite

These options solve different problems. The right choice depends on coverage confidence, site density, power limits, maintenance expectations, and whether the deployment can support additional local infrastructure.

## Cellular is usually strongest when

- Public coverage is credible at the site.
- Moderate bandwidth and straightforward deployment matter.
- The team wants fewer local network components to own.

## LoRaWAN is usually strongest when

- Asset density supports a local wireless network design.
- Low power and broad sensor distribution matter.
- The team can support gateway placement and network management.

## Satellite is usually strongest when

- Coverage confidence matters more than bandwidth cost.
- Remote access is rare and failure is expensive.
- The site profile makes terrestrial assumptions risky.
