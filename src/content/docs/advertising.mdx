Industrial Wireless I/O reserves commercial positions in the layout so future monetization can be introduced without redesigning the site or weakening trust.

## Reserved inventory model

Advertising is organized by page type rather than ad hoc decisions on individual pages. This creates a scalable operating model for a large technical reference site with many sections and categories.

## Large-site ad management

The site is designed to manage inventory by content profile:

- foundation pages carry the lightest commercial load;
- product-family and vendor pages support shortlist-stage inventory;
- application, network-path, and reliability pages support the highest-intent placements.

This profile-based structure is more manageable than tuning ads page by page as the library grows.

## Current status

Reserved commercial boxes may appear as placeholders. Their presence does not mean live paid campaigns are active across the site today.

## Editorial separation

Commercial placements should remain clearly labeled and visibly separate from technical analysis, comparisons, or recommendations.
