Hardware pages will organize the physical building blocks behind field telemetry systems and wireless industrial data collection.

## Focus

- Wireless I/O modules, gateways, routers, radios, and edge devices
- Supporting components such as antennas, enclosures, and power systems
- Hardware selection guidance linked to application and network conditions

This section is intended to help readers translate architecture plans into real device decisions.
