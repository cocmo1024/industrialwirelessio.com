Network path decisions determine cost, resilience, latency, and operational risk. This section will organize the transport side of remote industrial connectivity.

## Focus

- Cellular, radio, Ethernet, satellite, and mixed-path architectures
- Tradeoffs between coverage, cost, latency, and maintainability
- How backhaul choices affect telemetry design and device selection

These pages are intended to be practical planning tools rather than abstract networking notes.
