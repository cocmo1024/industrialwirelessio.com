# Industrial Telemetry Gateways

Telemetry gateways sit at the point where field signals, remote asset context, buffering, translation, and upstream transport all meet. They are often the architectural center of a remote monitoring site.

## What they usually do

- Collect, normalize, or buffer field data
- Translate between device protocols and upstream telemetry paths
- Provide local logic, diagnostics, or edge-aware resilience
