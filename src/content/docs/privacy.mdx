Industrial Wireless I/O is designed as a technical reference site. Limited information may be processed to deliver the site, protect it from abuse, and understand which field connectivity topics are most useful to readers.

## Information that may be collected

- Basic server or browser metadata
- Aggregate analytics related to page usage and navigation
- Information voluntarily submitted through email communication

## How information may be used

- Operate, secure, and improve the site
- Understand which telemetry, protocol, vendor, and hardware topics are most useful to readers
- Respond to editorial, correction, or commercial communication

## Third-party services

Hosting, analytics, or delivery infrastructure may process limited technical information as part of routine site operations. Their own privacy terms may also apply.

## Sensitive information

This site is not intended for confidential infrastructure data, site-security details, protected operational records, or sensitive personal information.
